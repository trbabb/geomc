3/*
4 * SimpleMatrix.h
5 *
6 * This involves a gambit very similar to the VecBase scenario:
7 * We have a class which is essentially unchanging in its body, but
8 * needs to have permuted constructors based on a template parameter.
9 * Therefore we put the entire body in a base class, and then make a thin
10 * derived class with partial specialization, and design the constructor
11 * interface as we please for each specialization.
12 *
13 * This class structure is pretty darn ugly, so we hide it from the user
14 * for the purposes of Doxygen by simply pretending the (hidden) common base class
15 * is the user-facing one.
16 *
17 * Created on: Apr 18, 2013
18 * Author: tbabb
19 */

30 what would we need for templated layout?
31
32 - tell us the row iterators
33 - tell us the column iterators
34 - provide get() --> {const,} iterator
35 - tell us how create storage
36 - tell us how to fill(?)
37
38 Would need to update generic row and column iterators to
39 iterate over the whole matrix, not just their own column.

42
